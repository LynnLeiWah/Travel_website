About Nay Pyi Taw

Naypyidaw is the capital city of Myanmar.
It was built in 2005 and is known for its wide streets and modern government buildings.
The city is much larger than most other cities in Myanmar but has fewer people.
Notable places include the Uppatasanti Pagoda, which resembles the Shwedagon Pagoda, and the National Museum. Naypyidaw was designed to be a planned city with specific zones for government, hotels, and shopping.

  • Zoological Gardens:A spacious zoo that houses a variety of animals, making it a fun spot for families and visitors.
